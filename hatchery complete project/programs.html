Our Programs

Comprehensive support for entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey

Turning Ideas into Successful Ventures

Bennett Hatchery offers a range of tailored programs designed to support entrepreneurs from the ideation stage to market expansion. Our structured approach ensures that startups receive the right guidance, resources, and opportunities at each phase of their development.

Whether you're just starting with an idea or looking to scale your existing venture, we have a program that fits your needs. Our goal is to maximize your chances of success by providing comprehensive support through mentorship, funding, workspace, and access to a vast network of industry experts and investors.

Our Program Categories

Tailored support based on your stage and needs

Ideation Program

Duration: 6-8 Weeks

The Ideation Program is designed for early-stage entrepreneurs who have an innovative idea but need guidance to validate it and develop a solid business model. Through structured workshops and mentorship, we help you refine your concept and prepare for the next stage of development.

Program Features

  • Idea validation workshops
  • Market research guidance
  • Business model canvas development
  • Pitch deck preparation
  • One-on-one mentoring sessions

Program Structure

  • Week 1-2: Idea validation and market analysis
  • Week 3-4: Business model development
  • Week 5-6: MVP planning and prototyping
  • Week 7-8: Pitch preparation and demo day

Acceleration Program

Duration: 4-6 Months

The Acceleration Program is tailored for startups that already have a product in the market and are looking to scale rapidly. We focus on growth strategies, market expansion, and connecting you with investors to secure larger funding rounds for scaling operations.

Program Features

  • Growth strategy development
  • Marketing and sales optimization
  • Direct access to investor network
  • Strategic partnerships with industry leaders
  • International market expansion support

Program Structure

  • Month 1: Assessment and strategy formulation
  • Month 2-3: Growth implementation and optimization
  • Month 4-5: Investor connections and funding preparation
  • Month 6: Demo day and graduation

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our programs

Who is eligible to apply for the programs?

Current Bennett University students and alumni can apply to all programs. For the Incubation and Acceleration Programs, we also accept applications from external entrepreneurs with innovative ideas or early-stage startups.

Is there a fee to participate in the programs?

The Ideation Program is free for all participants. For the Incubation and Acceleration Programs, we operate on an equity-based model where we take a small equity stake (typically 2-5%) in exchange for the funding, resources, and support provided.

What is the selection process like?

The selection process includes an initial application screening, followed by multiple rounds of interviews and pitch presentations. We evaluate ideas based on innovation, market potential, team capabilities, and alignment with our focus areas.

How many startups do you accept per batch?

We typically accept 15-20 teams for the Ideation Program, 8-10 startups for the Incubation Program, and 5-7 startups for the Acceleration Program per batch. This allows us to provide focused attention and resources to each participant.

When are the application deadlines?

We run multiple batches throughout the year. The Ideation Program has quarterly intakes, while the Incubation and Acceleration Programs have biannual intakes. Check the Apply page for current application deadlines.
