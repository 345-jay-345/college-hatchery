About Bennett Hatchery

Nurturing innovation and empowering student entrepreneurs at Bennett University

Our Story

Bennett Hatchery was established in 2017 with a vision to transform innovative ideas from students into successful businesses. As the startup incubator at Bennett University, we aim to foster entrepreneurial thinking and provide comprehensive support to young entrepreneurs.

What began as a small initiative has grown into a thriving ecosystem of mentors, investors, and entrepreneurs working together to create impactful startups. Today, we've successfully incubated over 50 startups across various sectors and continue to expand our programs and reach.

Our Mission

To cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset among students and provide them with the resources, mentorship, and support needed to transform innovative ideas into successful businesses.

Our Vision

To be the premier university-based startup incubator in India, creating a robust ecosystem that nurtures innovation and entrepreneurship among young minds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Bennett Hatchery

What is Bennett Hatchery?

Bennett Hatchery is the startup incubator at Bennett University. We provide support, resources, mentorship, and funding opportunities to student entrepreneurs and innovators looking to build successful startups.

Do I need to be a Bennett University student to apply?

While our primary focus is on Bennett University students, we do accept applications from external entrepreneurs for specific programs. Check the eligibility criteria for each program on our Programs page.

What kind of support does Bennett Hatchery provide?

We offer a comprehensive support system including workspace, mentorship from industry experts, technical guidance, legal support, networking opportunities, access to investors, and seed funding for selected startups.

How can I get involved if I'm not an entrepreneur?

There are many ways to get involved! You can become a mentor, volunteer at events, partner with us, or invest in our startups. Reach out to us through the Contact page to explore collaboration opportunities.
