The Bennett Hatchery Mentorship Program

Our mentors are the backbone of Bennett Hatchery, providing valuable guidance, industry insights, and connections to our startups. Each mentor brings years of experience across various domains and industries, offering personalized support to help founders navigate the challenges of building a successful business.

Through one-on-one mentoring sessions, workshops, and industry connections, our mentors help startups refine their business models, develop go-to-market strategies, secure funding, and scale their operations.

Our Mentorship Process

How we connect startups with the right mentors

1

Needs Assessment

We begin by understanding your startup's specific needs, challenges, and goals to identify the areas where mentorship would be most valuable.

2

Mentor Matching

Based on your needs, we match you with mentors who have relevant expertise and experience in your industry or domain.

3

Structured Sessions

Regular one-on-one mentoring sessions are scheduled with clear objectives and action items to ensure productive engagement.

4

Progress Tracking

We track your progress against defined goals and adjust the mentorship plan as your startup evolves and faces new challenges.

Become a Mentor

Share your expertise and experience with the next generation of entrepreneurs. Join our mentor network and help shape the future of innovation at Bennett University.

Why Become a Mentor?

  • Give back to the entrepreneurial ecosystem
  • Stay connected with emerging technologies and business models
  • Expand your professional network
  • Potential investment and partnership opportunities
  • Personal and professional growth

Mentor Expectations:

We ask our mentors to commit to at least 4 hours per month for a minimum of 6 months. This includes one-on-one mentoring sessions, group workshops, and occasional events.
