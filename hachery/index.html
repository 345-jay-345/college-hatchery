Bennett University

Bennett University has been set up at Greater Noida and commenced operations on 08 Aug 2016, initially with B.Tech and MBA programs. With a focus on giving students a premium learning experience, in an immersive environment at a campus designed by the internationally renowned RSP Architects, coupled with enhanced use of technology to meet students' aspirations.

About the Hatchery

The Hatchery at Bennett University is a startup incubator designed to support student entrepreneurs in transforming their ideas into successful ventures. We provide mentorship, funding opportunities, and access to a vast network of investors and industry leaders.
